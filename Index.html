Off-White x Nike Presto Is Releasing In July And August
Of all the sequels to the Off-White x Nike “The Ten Icons” releases, the Presto might be the one the collective world of sneakers fans is looking forward to the most. Virgil’s deconstructed aesthetic shines brightest on this light-weight lifestyle running shoe, and this Fall Nike will finally drop the much-anticipated follow-up to the shoe many deemed the best of 2017. It appears that the black colorway will officially drop on July 27th, while the white version will be available on August 3rd. Keep your sneaker release dates calendars up to date with this development and stay tuned for more Off-White x Nike news.
